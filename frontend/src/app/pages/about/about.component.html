Our Journey

We believe that coffee is more than a beverage; it’s an experience.

2015 - The Inception

Started in a small garage with a 1kg roaster and a dream to ethically source the best beans.

2018 - First Flagship

Opened our first BrewLive café, bringing artisanal coffee directly to the local community.

2021 - Going Digital

Launched our app to make our premium roasts accessible nationwide with 24-hour delivery.

2024 - Global Sourcing

Direct-trade partnerships established with over 15 farms across Ethiopia, Colombia, and Costa Rica.
